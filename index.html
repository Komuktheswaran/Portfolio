About Me

I'm a Full-Stack Developer and Data Analyst with a Master's in Computer Applications from MEPCO Schlenk Engineering College, Sivakasi. Over the past year, I've built and shipped applications that real businesses rely on every single day — not just portfolio pieces or hobby projects.

One of my proudest builds is MAAST — a workforce attendance and shift management platform running inside a 500+ employee manufacturing factory. It handles everything from punch data ingestion and automated shift assignments to employee swap workflows and real-time reporting across React and SQL Server. I also built Vasantham Crackers World from the ground up — a full-stack chit fund platform for a cracker shop that manages customer enrollment, monthly scheme schedules, partial payments, auctions, bulk payments, and separate access for Admin and Staff. Both of these are live systems processing real financial data every day.

On the mobile side, I published TagDial on the Google Play Store — an Android app that shows caller info on both locked and unlocked screens using Android's Overlay Service and Call Log API, built natively in Java with Android Studio. I also built Inprocess Check, a shop-floor production logging system for a manufacturing unit (React Native + Express + SQL Server), and Foundry, a heat batch and procurement tracking app for a foundry business (Kotlin + Java).

Outside of factory and manufacturing tools, I developed a Vehicle Service Management System in Django that covers the full service lifecycle — bookings, job cards, technician assignments, billing, and inventory management. I also built a Task Management app tailored for tax-filing compliance workflows, giving both staff and customers real-time visibility into job progress. When it comes to deployment, I'm comfortable shipping on Windows Server (IIS / iisnode) as well as AWS.
